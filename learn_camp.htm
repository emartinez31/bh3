Scout and Campers

A Victorian Tea

This program invites youngsters to participate in a popular ritual of a by-gone era. It is used to introduce children to etiquette of the early 1900s while learning about other aspects of community life. Attendees are requested to dress for the occasion. This program is designed for up to 15 students and is conducted on week days only.

Program Fee: $15 per person. Duration: 90 minutes

Recommended Grades: 6 - 12
